With his sultry, suave voice

AVAZ is a natural tenor who utilizes multiple ranges to create a vocal dynamic unique to his chosen genres. Hailing from Hamburg, Germany, AVAZ is proficient in many instruments which he brings to every project with a style and definition allowing him to blend extraordinary sounds.

With his two released singles, “Sinner” and “Wasted Love”, AVAZ is gathering a steadily increasing following. Many of his fans appreciate the subtle ways he approaches his music with a defined clarity.
